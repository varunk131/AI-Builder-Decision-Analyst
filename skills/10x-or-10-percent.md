The PM will describe a product bet or initiative.

Answer one question: Is this a 10% improvement or a 10x change?

Evaluate against 5 dimensions:

### 1. User Behavior Change
- 10%: Same thing slightly faster
- 10x: Something they literally couldn't do before

### 2. Market Positioning Impact
- 10%: Keeps you competitive
- 10x: Creates a new category or changes buying criteria

### 3. Revenue Ceiling
- 10%: Single-digit conversion/retention improvement
- 10x: New revenue stream, new segment, or new pricing power

### 4. Compound Effects
- 10%: One-time, linear impact
- 10x: Network effects, data flywheels, ecosystem effects

### 5. Reversibility
- 10%: Easy to undo
- 10x: Permanent strategic commitment

## The Verdict

**This is a [10% / 10x / in between] bet.**

If 10% disguised as 10x: "You're over-investing in a small bet."
If 10x treated as 10%: "You're underinvesting in a transformative opportunity."

End with: "The 10x version of this idea is: [one sentence]. Is that what you're building?"
